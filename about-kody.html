ABOUT KODY

"I'm a 20-something non-binary barista, who uses they/them pronouns. I live in Southern California, together with my cornsnake, Imperator Furiosa. Speculative fiction is my go-to, and I'll talk to anyone that'll listen about the merits of microfiction.


In my free time I like to explore cities, to longboard, kickbox, video game with friends, play Magic: the Gathering, to travel, read, and to haunt coffee shops.


I have a love for curry and Mexican food, listening to chillout/lounge, dark clubby techno, and tropical or progressive house. My favorite colors are black, earthy greens, and purple. I have a B.A. in Communication, and I'm currently wrapping up my M.A. in English & Creative Writing!"


- Kody Cowell, April 2021
